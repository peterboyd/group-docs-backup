Linux Mint 13 Xfce

32 bits or 64 bits :

 

Under 4Gb of ram 32 bits is better, at 4Gb or better, use 64 bits or at least a 32 bits PAE kernel that will allow to use the ram over 3,5Gb

 

Easy suggestion to make it even more lightweight and fast :

 

sudo apt-get install openbox obconf

Then follow this tutorial :

http://openbox.org/wiki/Help:XFCE/Openbox

 

Troubleshooting advice :

 

Use google and seek for keywords related to your problem and the word Ubuntu, Ubuntu being the base distro of Mint, this will give out more results to help you ...

 

Use Google translate to seek for the amazing french documention of Ubuntu that may be fairly decent for beginners also ...

 

http://doc.ubuntu-fr.org/
