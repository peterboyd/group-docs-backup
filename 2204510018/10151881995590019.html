    HISTORY

    ======

    You like Linux, right? Of course, you do! Otherwise, you wouldn't be here. You're using the system everyday. You probably know the in's and out's of it by now. But do you know how it came to be, how it all got started, and why? The history of Linux and everything connected to it represents a long and arduous journey in the realms of computer science, engineering, commerce, legality, culture, community, and freedom.
